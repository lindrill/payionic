Your Privacy

Affinity Games is committed to protecting your privacy. We only use the information we collect about you to process orders and to provide support and upgrades for our products. Please read on for more details about our privacy policy.

What information do we collect? How do we use it?

Affinity Games respects the privacy rights of its customers. With that said, personal information like name, e-mail address, mailing address, credit card number, and expiration date will only be collected for the purpose of filling orders and to notify its customers of its status.

When a customer enters a contest, we may ask for a name, address, and e-mail address in order for us to notify winners.

By registering for our site, you agree to be added to our mailing list for future promotions and deals.

When you order through our website, you are opting to receive newsletters about coupons, promotions, and important announcements.

E-mails are sent via ConstantContact, so you may choose to opt out if you no longer want to receive newsletters from us or our MTG partners.

How does Affinity Games protect customer information?

Affinity Games protects the privacy of its customers due to the nature of sensitivity of the information being collected.

Our site uses SSL (Secure Server Layer) that helps scramble all of the information that you enter before you send it to us. This ensures that all data transmitted is protected against unauthorized access.

Will Affinity Games disclose the information it collects to outside parties?

Affinity Games will not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to individuals/organizations not related to Affinity Games. We may provide aggregate statistics about our customers, sales, traffic patterns, and related site information to reputable third party vendors, but these statistics will not show personal identifying information.

Affinity Games may release account information when we believe, in good faith, that the release of such information is necessary to (a) to comply with the law, (b) enforce or apply terms of any of our user agreements, or (c) protect the rights, property, and safety of Affinity Games, our users, and others.

In Summary

Affinity Games is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site to make shopping at affinitygames.crystalcommerce.com as simple as possible and to enhance your overall shopping experience. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
